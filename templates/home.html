Musync

Musync allows musicians to synthesize individual recordings into one complete performance so that musical groups can perform together virtually. Whether you play in an orchestra, band, chamber group, or simply with a friend, Musync will allow you to produce a stunning musical performance without having to physically meet up.

Combine

Musync combines individual parts by synchronizing a starting “clap” in each recording. Once overlaid, the final audio makes it seem as if the whole group was playing in the same room!

Review

Music instructors and conductors can use the finalized video to provide useful educational feedback. For example, the video could help the conductor check for problems with bowing, posture, intonation, rhythm, or balance.
